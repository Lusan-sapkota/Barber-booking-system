Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Appointments

To book an appointment:

  1. Click "Book Appointment" in the navigation or homepage
  2. Select your preferred service type
  3. Choose a date and available time slot
  4. Fill in your contact information
  5. Confirm your booking

You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment:

  • Cancellation: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your appointment
  • Rescheduling: You can reschedule up to 2 hours before your appointment
  • Use your customer dashboard or contact us directly

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Cash payment at the shop
Account Management

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  2. Choose your account type (Customer, Barber, or Shop Owner)
  3. Fill in your personal information
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile setup

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password
